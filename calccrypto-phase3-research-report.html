Risk & Compliance Checklist

Legal Disclaimers

  • All outputs labelled "estimates only, not financial advice"
  • Tax estimator: "Generic rules only — consult a qualified accountant"
  • Stablecoin data: "Data sourced externally; verify with issuer directly"
  • Portfolio tracker: "For informational purposes only"

Affiliate Disclosures

  • Clear "Sponsored" or "Affiliate" label on all referral links
  • FCA/ASA compliant disclosure for UK audience
  • ASA (US) compliant disclosure for US audience
  • No endorsement language; factual comparison only

Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA)

  • No server-side user data storage
  • localStorage use: opt-in notice, clear data button
  • Cookie consent banner for Coinzilla / AdSense
  • Privacy Policy updated to cover analytics + ad cookies

ToS & Jurisdictional Notes

  • ToS updated to cover new calculator outputs
  • Tax estimator: explicit caveat per jurisdiction shown
  • Note Coinbase affiliate program restrictions for UK users
  • Airdrop checker: no financial outcome guarantees
