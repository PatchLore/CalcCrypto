## Planning a Dollar-Cost Averaging Strategy

One of the most common investor questions during a correction is:

"Should I buy now or wait?"

Trying to perfectly time a market bottom is notoriously difficult. Instead, many investors use Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) to spread purchases over time.
