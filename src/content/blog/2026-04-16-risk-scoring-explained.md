# How We Detect Crypto Risks (Coming Soon)

As we prepare to launch Phase 2 of CalCrypto, we're excited to share some insights into how our risk detection system will work. This isn't about market prediction—it's about providing clear, actionable risk intelligence.

## Understanding Risk in Crypto

Crypto risk comes in many forms:

- **Smart contract vulnerabilities**
- **Liquidity issues**
- **Team and project health**
- **Market manipulation**
- **Regulatory changes**
- **Technical problems**

Traditional risk assessment often relies on subjective analysis or expensive data feeds. We're building something different: deterministic, transparent risk scoring that anyone can understand and verify.

## Our Risk Assessment Framework

### 1. On-Chain Metrics
We analyze blockchain data to understand token health:

- **Transaction volume patterns**
- **Holder distribution**
- **Contract activity**
- **Liquidity depth**

### 2. Market Structure Analysis
Understanding how tokens trade:

- **Order book depth**
- **Slippage analysis**
- **Trading pair stability**
- **Exchange concentration**

### 3. Project Fundamentals
Evaluating the underlying project:

- **Code quality indicators**
- **Development activity**
- **Community health**
- **Documentation completeness**

## Deterministic Scoring

Unlike other risk tools that use complex algorithms or AI, our system provides:

- **Clear scoring criteria**: Every factor is transparent
- **Reproducible results**: Same data always produces the same score
- **Weighted calculations**: Understand how each factor contributes
- **Regular updates**: Fresh data ensures current assessments

## Integration with Calculators

The real power comes when risk intelligence integrates with our calculators:

- **Position sizing** with risk context
- **Profit/loss** calculations with risk warnings
- **Portfolio analysis** with risk heatmaps
- **Tax calculations** with risk-adjusted strategies

## Privacy and Security First

Just like our calculators, the risk intelligence features will:

- Run entirely client-side
- Never collect user data
- Provide deterministic results
- Require no signup or authentication

## Coming Soon

We're currently in the final testing phase of Phase 2. The risk intelligence features will initially integrate with our Token Price calculator, providing real-time risk assessment for any token you analyze.

This is just the beginning. As we expand, we'll bring risk intelligence to more of our tools, helping traders make better-informed decisions in the complex world of cryptocurrency.
